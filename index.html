Ferris Bueller's Day Off

Last Favourite Movie

My favourite that I've watched recently has to be "Ferris Bueller's Day Off." I watched it for the first time with my papa because we wanted to spend more quality time together! I really enjoyed the movie because it was so so silly and unrealistic but at the same time felt realistic. My papa and I we're laughing the whole time and after we talked about how he used to pull a "Ferris Bueller" with his parents. The movie itself is very good and funny but, what enhances my experience more is the association it has to my papa!
